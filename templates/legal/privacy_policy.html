Your Privacy Matters

At EduSight, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information about you when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password, phone number, country
  • Profile Information: User type (parent, student, educator), preferences, settings
  • Contact Information: When you contact us for support or inquiries
Educational Data
  • Academic Records: Report cards, test scores, grades, assessment results
  • Student Information: Grade level, subjects, curriculum type, academic history
  • Performance Data: Analysis results, predictions, recommendations
Technical Information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision
  • Generate personalized assessments and insights
  • Provide AI-powered educational recommendations
  • Create performance tracking and analytics
  • Facilitate parent-student progress monitoring
Platform Improvement
  • Enhance machine learning algorithms (anonymized data)
  • Improve assessment accuracy and relevance
  • Develop new features and functionalities
  • Conduct research and development activities
Communication
  • Send important account and service updates
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Share educational insights and recommendations (if consented)
  • Send marketing communications (with explicit consent only)

3. Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data backup and recovery systems
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures
  • Compliance monitoring and reporting
Data Storage and Retention
  • Location: Data stored in secure, compliant data centers
  • Retention Period: Account data retained while account is active
  • Educational Data: Retained for analysis and historical tracking purposes
  • Deletion Policy: Data deleted upon account closure request (subject to legal requirements)

4. Information Sharing

We Do NOT Share Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

Permitted Sharing
  • With Consent: When you explicitly authorize information sharing
  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who assist in service delivery (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety Protection: To protect the safety and security of our users
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale (with user notification)
Anonymized Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify individual users for:

  • Educational research and development
  • Industry trend analysis
  • Algorithm improvement initiatives
  • Academic partnerships and collaborations

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access Rights
  • View your personal information
  • Download your data (data portability)
  • Access assessment history and results
  • Review account activity logs
Control Rights
  • Correct or update personal information
  • Delete account and associated data
  • Restrict certain data processing
  • Object to automated decision-making
Communication Preferences
  • Opt-out of marketing emails
  • Customize notification settings
  • Choose communication frequency
  • Select preferred communication channels

EU General Data Protection Regulation compliance for European users

COPPA

FERPA

Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act compliance for educational records

8. Children's Privacy

Special Protection for Minors

We take special care to protect the privacy of children and students:

  • Parental consent required for users under 13
  • Limited data collection for educational purposes only
  • No marketing communications to children
  • Enhanced security measures for student data
  • Regular review of children's data handling practices

9. International Data Transfers

If we transfer your data internationally, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
  • Explicit consent for specific transfers

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our services or business practices
  • New legal or regulatory requirements
  • Enhanced privacy protection measures
  • User feedback and suggestions

We will notify you of significant changes through: Email notifications, in-platform announcements, and website notices at least 30 days before changes take effect.
